Detailed instructions on how to use the cape site

Step 1: Go to the Hypixel server.
Step 2: Press the right Shift key.
Step 3: Click on the "Next >" button.
Step 4: Click on the "Web profile" button.
Step 5: Click on the "Open a web profile" button.
Step 6: Looking at the result.

Step 1: Click on the "Edit" button.
Step 2: Enter the name of the person you want to borrow the cape from.
Step 3: Click on the "Get info" button.
Step 4: Click on the "None" button, if nothing happens, the player has no cape.
Step 5: Choose what you like.
Step 6: Click on the "Save" button.
Step 7: Click on the "Select" button to select the TNTClient cape.
Step 8: Click on the "Save" button and wait five minutes.

Optional  Step 10: I know that maths is difficult, but next comes the formulas. We need to determine which formula we should use. If width > height, we use formula 1, and if width < height, we use formula 2.
Optional  Step 11: Calculate the dimensions of the image.

If you have formula 1, it looks like this: Width=2*Height/3. You need to substitute the height in the formula and get the width. Use a calculator for these calculations, round up.

If you have formula 2, it looks like this: Height=3*Width/2. You need to substitute the width in the formula and get the height. Use a calculator for these calculations, round up.
